Our Product Warranty and Quality Guarantee

Warranty Information

Last Updated: January 2025

At JBR7 Bags Manufacturing, we stand behind the quality of our products. This warranty information outlines our commitment to providing you with durable, high-quality bags and our policies for addressing any manufacturing defects.

Warranty Coverage

We provide warranty coverage for the following:

  • Manufacturing Defects: Defects in materials or workmanship
  • Structural Issues: Broken zippers, torn seams, or hardware failures
  • Material Defects: Fabric tears, color fading (beyond normal wear), or material deterioration
  • Quality Issues: Products that do not meet our quality standards

Warranty Period

Our warranty periods vary by product type:

  • Standard Products: 30 days from date of purchase
  • Custom Orders: 30 days from date of delivery (manufacturing defects only)
  • Bulk Orders: 30 days from date of delivery

All warranty claims must be reported within the warranty period.

What's Covered

The warranty covers:

  • Manufacturing defects in materials or workmanship
  • Defective zippers, buckles, or hardware
  • Torn seams or stitching defects
  • Material defects (tears, holes, premature wear)
  • Printing or logo defects (for custom orders)

What's Not Covered

The warranty does NOT cover:

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage from misuse or abuse
  • Damage from accidents or improper care
  • Damage from exposure to extreme conditions
  • Modifications or alterations made by the customer
  • Cosmetic issues that don't affect functionality
  • Products used beyond their intended purpose

Warranty Claims Process

To file a warranty claim:

  • Step 1: Contact us immediately with photos of the defect
  • Step 2: Provide your order number and purchase date
  • Step 3: Our team will review your claim within 2-3 business days
  • Step 4: If approved, we'll provide a replacement or full refund
  • Step 5: Defective items may not need to be returned (unless requested)

Warranty Remedies

If your product is covered under warranty, we will:

  • Replace: Provide a new product of the same model (if available)
  • Refund: Issue a full refund of the purchase price
  • Repair: Repair the defect when possible (at our discretion)

We reserve the right to choose the most appropriate remedy based on the nature of the defect and product availability.

Shipping for Warranty Claims

For approved warranty claims:

  • Replacement shipping is free of charge
  • Return shipping for defective items is covered by us
  • We provide prepaid shipping labels when returns are required
  • International customers may be responsible for return shipping costs

Quality Guarantee

Beyond our warranty, we guarantee:

  • All products meet our quality standards before shipping
  • Thorough quality inspection before delivery
  • Commitment to customer satisfaction
  • Continuous improvement of our manufacturing processes

Care Instructions

To maintain your bag and prevent issues:

  • Follow care instructions provided with your product
  • Avoid overloading beyond recommended capacity
  • Store in a dry, cool place when not in use
  • Clean according to material-specific guidelines
  • Handle zippers and hardware with care
